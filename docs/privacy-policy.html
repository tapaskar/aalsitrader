Privacy Policy for AalsiTrader

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Introduction

AalsiTrader ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application AalsiTrader (the "App").

Information We Collect

The App requires minimal permissions and collects limited data:

  • Internet Access: The App requires internet connectivity to communicate with our backend servers for trading data, portfolio updates, and real-time market information.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email address and login credentials to authenticate your sessions.
  • Trading Data: Paper trading activity, portfolio state, trade history, and performance metrics are stored on our servers to provide the App's functionality.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone.
  • We do not collect personal financial or brokerage account information for paper trading mode.
  • We do not serve third-party advertisements or use ad-tracking SDKs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's trading dashboard and paper trading features.
  • Display your portfolio, trade history, equity curve, and performance analytics.
  • Send real-time updates via WebSocket connections.
  • Improve and optimize the App's performance and user experience.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting. These services may collect information used to identify you. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
